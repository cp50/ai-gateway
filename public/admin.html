What This Dashboard Proves

This product is an AI control plane, not a wrapper around one model.

It routes between models, tracks health over time, persists telemetry in Redis, and exposes an operational view that makes failure, cache behavior, and latency visible. This is the piece most people miss when they build AI apps.
